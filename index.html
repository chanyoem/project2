Don't lose your spark!

What is burnout? Burnout is reaching the point of complete mental, physical, and emotional exhaustion. When you completely lose sight of yourself and what you are working towards. The feeling of being tired of even existing. Once you're in it, it often feels like there is no way out, but this experience is something most people go through. You are never alone, so don't lose hope. Pick a flame to possibly help light your path!

Need a light?

Ways to prevent burnout:

  • When you are starting to feel unmotivate, slow down and take a break and be patient with yourself
  • Prioritise your health and physical needs such as sleep, eating, staying hydrated, and being active
  • Have a change of work environment

Spot the smoke!

How to recognize the signs of burnout:

  • Constantly tired and unmotivated
  • Feeling that what you do is not important
  • Being highly irritable over smaller things
  • Feeling stuck and procrastinating

Only YOU can prevent burnout!

How to manage burnout:

  • Be patient with yourself and give yourself rest, you come first and you can't do anything without the energy
  • Sit with your feelings and acknowledge that what you're experiencing isn't abnormal and you will feel better in time
  • Spend some time outside or change up your routine to add some excitement
